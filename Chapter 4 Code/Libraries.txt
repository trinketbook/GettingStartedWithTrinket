The following third-party libraries are believed to work with Trinket:

* SendOnlySoftwareSerial--by Nick Gammon http://forum.arduino.cc/index.php?topic=112013.0
* Adafruit_SoftServo-- https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit_SoftServo[https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit_SoftServo] with a tutorial at http://learn.adafruit.com/trinket-gemma-servo-control/code
* TinyWireM-- https://github.com/adafruit/TinyWireM
* TinyWireS-- https://github.com/rambo/TinyWire
* TinyLiquidCrystal-- https://github.com/adafruit/TinyLiquidCrystal
* Adafruit_Neopixel-- https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit_NeoPixel
* LastLED-- https://github.com/FastLED/FastLED
* Adafruit_LEDBackpack-- https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit-LED-Backpack-Library]
* Adafruit_GFX-- https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit-GFX-Library
* TinyAdafruit_RGBLCDShield-- http://learn.adafruit.com/trinket-rgb-shield-clock/overview 
* TinyDHT-- https://github.com/adafruit/TinyDHT
* TrinketKeyboard-- https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit-Trinket-USB/
* TrinketHidCombo-- https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit-Trinket-USB/
* TinyRTClib-- https://github.com/adafruit/TinyRTCLib
* Adafruit_TinyFlash-- https://github.com/adafruit/Adafruit_TinyFlash/
* TinyNarcoleptic-- http://forums.adafruit.com/viewtopic.php?f=52&t=47423
* Servo8Bit-- https://github.com/solderspot/Servo8Bit
* tinySPI--by Jack Christensen https://github.com/JChristensen/tinySPI
* Arduino-UsiSerial--by Frank Zhao https://github.com/frank26080115/Arduino-UsiSerial-Library/
* VirtualWire-- http://www.airspayce.com/mikem/arduino/VirtualWire/
* TinyNewPing--by Matthew http://projectsfromtech.blogspot.com/2014/01/i2c-hc-sr04-sonar-module-attiny85-i2c.html
